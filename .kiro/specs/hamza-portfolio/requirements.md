# Requirements Document

## Introduction

A modern, professional portfolio website for Hamza, a 17-year-old web and game developer from Pakistan. The portfolio will showcase multiple projects including super-team.vercel.app, zypher-cloud.vercel.app, cute-cloud.vercel.app, and gamer-grove.vercel.app with a cohesive design theme, smooth animations, and professional presentation.

## Glossary

- **Portfolio_System**: The complete portfolio website application
- **Project_Showcase**: Individual project display components with descriptions and links
- **Theme_Engine**: The consistent color and design system applied across all components
- **Animation_System**: Motion and transition effects throughout the website
- **Navigation_Component**: Main site navigation and routing system
- **Contact_Interface**: Methods for visitors to contact Hamza

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a potential client or employer, I want to view Hamza's portfolio, so that I can assess his skills and previous work.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display Hamza's name, age (17), and location (Pakistan) prominently on the homepage
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL showcase at least four projects: super-team.vercel.app, zypher-cloud.vercel.app, cute-cloud.vercel.app, and gamer-grove.vercel.app
3. WHEN a visitor accesses the portfolio, THE Portfolio_System SHALL load within 3 seconds
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL be responsive and display correctly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include a professional bio highlighting web and game development expertise

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to navigate through different sections of the portfolio, so that I can explore Hamza's work and background.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Navigation_Component SHALL provide access to Home, About, Projects, and Contact sections
2. WHEN a user clicks on a navigation item, THE Portfolio_System SHALL smoothly transition to the selected section
3. THE Navigation_Component SHALL remain accessible and visible on all pages
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL highlight the current active section in the navigation
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL support both click and keyboard navigation

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to see detailed information about each project, so that I can understand Hamza's technical capabilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Project_Showcase SHALL display project title, description, and live demo link for each project
2. WHEN a user hovers over a project, THE Animation_System SHALL provide visual feedback
3. THE Project_Showcase SHALL include technology stack information for each project
4. THE Project_Showcase SHALL provide working links to live project demos
5. THE Project_Showcase SHALL display projects in an organized, visually appealing grid layout

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to experience smooth, professional animations, so that the portfolio feels modern and engaging.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Animation_System SHALL provide smooth page transitions between sections
2. WHEN elements come into view, THE Animation_System SHALL animate them with subtle entrance effects
3. THE Animation_System SHALL include hover effects on interactive elements
4. THE Animation_System SHALL maintain 60fps performance during all animations
5. THE Animation_System SHALL respect user preferences for reduced motion when applicable

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to see a cohesive visual design, so that the portfolio appears professional and well-crafted.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Theme_Engine SHALL use a consistent color palette throughout the entire website
2. THE Theme_Engine SHALL apply the same typography system across all components
3. THE Theme_Engine SHALL maintain consistent spacing and layout patterns
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL avoid conflicting colors and maintain visual harmony
5. THE Theme_Engine SHALL use a single font family with varying weights and styles

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a potential client, I want to contact Hamza easily, so that I can discuss potential projects or opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Contact_Interface SHALL provide multiple contact methods (email, social media links)
2. THE Contact_Interface SHALL be easily accessible from any page
3. THE Contact_Interface SHALL include a contact form for direct messaging
4. WHEN a user submits the contact form, THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide confirmation feedback
5. THE Contact_Interface SHALL display Hamza's professional social media profiles

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to experience a cinematic journey through Hamza's portfolio, so that I feel emotionally connected and remember the experience long after leaving the site.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL create a continuous, film-like narrative that flows from introduction to conclusion without jarring transitions
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL use camera-cut style transitions between sections that focus, reveal, then guide the eye naturally
3. WHEN scrolling through the portfolio, THE Animation_System SHALL create rhythm by mixing high-energy moments with calmer, breathing spaces
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make visitors feel like they're watching a short film about Hamza's journey, not browsing a website
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL leave visitors with a lasting emotional impression of confidence, creativity, and professionalism

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to feel the portfolio is alive and breathing, so that every element feels intentionally placed with emotional purpose rather than following templates.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL use whitespace as "silence" - creating breathing room that makes content feel more impactful
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL implement subtle organic curves or diagonal elements to break rigid rectangular layouts
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL create depth through light, spacing, and shadows rather than layered floating boxes
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include a unique visual signature (recurring glow, ripple, or pattern) that feels distinctly like Hamza's brand
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make every visual choice feel deliberate and meaningful, not decorative

### Requirement 9

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to feel the emotional impact of carefully chosen typography and colors, so that the design feels warm, confident, and personally crafted.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Theme_Engine SHALL use maximum two typefaces with personality - clean and modern with subtle character in headers
2. THE Theme_Engine SHALL implement a tight, muted color palette with one standout accent color for emotional impact
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL use warm tones for active/interactive elements and cool neutrals for comfortable backgrounds
4. THE Theme_Engine SHALL create emotional connection through color choices rather than just visual appeal
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make typography feel like motion type that builds in naturally, not flies in mechanically

### Requirement 10

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want to experience motion that feels natural and purposeful, so that animations enhance the story rather than distract from it.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Animation_System SHALL use motion at half the intensity typically expected - subtle and refined
2. THE Animation_System SHALL implement slow parallax and smooth easing that creates depth without distraction  
3. WHEN elements enter view, THE Animation_System SHALL use gentle fade and rise animations that feel like breathing
4. THE Animation_System SHALL create rhythm through deliberate pacing - not constant motion
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make visitors forget about the animations and remember the feeling instead

### Requirement 11

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want each section to feel like a distinct chapter with its own personality, so that the portfolio tells a cohesive story with varied emotional beats.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL design the hero section to be cinematic with slow, breathing motion and minimal, well-placed words
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL present projects like individual stage moments - each deserving its own spotlight with deliberate pacing
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make the about section feel like a personal story unfolding in real-time through scroll
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL create a contact section that feels quiet, focused, and warmly approachable
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL ensure each section has distinct energy levels that create natural rhythm throughout the experience

### Requirement 12

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want interactions to feel meaningful and restrained, so that every hover and click confirms the thoughtful craftsmanship behind the portfolio.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Animation_System SHALL include hover effects that are meaningful - light sweeps, gentle lifts, or background movements that hint at depth
2. THE Animation_System SHALL use gentle transitions that confirm interaction without overreacting
3. THE Animation_System SHALL implement subtle glows or gradient shifts for warmth and approachability
4. THE Animation_System SHALL create expanding cards or soft transitions for content reveals
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL make all interactions feel calm, confident, and precisely controlled

### Requirement 13

**User Story:** As a visitor using assistive technology, I want to navigate and understand the portfolio content, so that I can access all information regardless of my abilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include proper semantic HTML structure
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide alt text for all images and graphics
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL support keyboard navigation for all interactive elements
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL maintain sufficient color contrast ratios for text readability
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include proper ARIA labels and roles where necessary
